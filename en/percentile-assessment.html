Percentile Assessment Service

Professional assessment of your child's height, weight, and head circumference. Growth indicator monitoring and ensuring healthy development.

What is a Percentile?

Percentile is a statistical measure that shows how your child's measurements (height, weight, head circumference) compare to other children of the same age and gender. This indicator helps assess whether the child's growth and development are normal.

For example, if your child's weight is in the 75th percentile, it means that 75% of children of the same age and gender weigh less than your child. This indicates healthy growth indicators for your child.

Regular Check-ups

✓ Every 3 months (0-2 years)

✓ Annually (6 years and above)
✓ Before starting school
✓ When there are concerns about growth
